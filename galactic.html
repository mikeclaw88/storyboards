3025 Battle of Football

50th Anniversary Broadcast Script

NARRATOR:
"The 3025 Battle of Football is proud to celebrate its 50th completion. Since the darkness fell in 2042, we’ve strived to push the boundaries of intergalactic football. Pick your favorite... and cheer them to glory!"

1. Terran United (Humans)

"For the Home World... The King returns."
Shot A: A crumbling, overgrown Maracanã. The crowd holds flares forming "HOPE".

Shot B: Pelé, glowing in yellow, levitates with a ball of pure light. He is the warm heart of a cold galaxy.

2. Neon Soul FC (Androids)

"Perfection has a name. Optimization complete."
Shot A: Cyberpunk locker room. Cables disconnect from players' necks.

Shot B: Cristiano Ronaldo, metallic skin, glowing cyan eyes. He performs the "Siuuu", shattering digital glass around him.

3. Red Sands Athletic (Martians)

"Gravity cannot hold a God."
Shot A: A stadium carved into a red Martian canyon. 20ft high goalposts.

Shot B: Zlatan, in rusty crimson armor, executing a bicycle kick 15ft in the air.

4. The Order XI (Jedi)

"The Force flows through the ball."
Shot A: Floating temple in the clouds. Silent monk crowd.

Shot B: Messi in robes, meditating. The ball orbits him telekinetically before launching at lightspeed.

5. Cylon Centurions (Drones)

"Relentless. Unforgiving. Keane."
Shot A: Factory assembly line marching onto a steel pitch.

Shot B: Roy Keane, half-machine, red cybernetic eye scanning for weakness.

6. Na'vi Blue Waves

"The rhythm of the wild."
Shot A: Bioluminescent jungle at night.

Shot B: Ronaldinho, blue-skinned, swinging from vines, catching the ball with a massive grin.

7. The Hive Mind (Xenomorph)

"The hunger never ends."
Shot A: Slimy organic ship interior. Walls breathing.

Shot B: Luis Suárez with an elongated alien headpiece, sprinting on all fours, striking with lethal power.

8. The Vulcan Logic

"Calculate. Execute. Win."
Shot A: Sterile, geometric glass field in deep space.

Shot B: Kevin De Bruyne adjusting a holographic visor, calculating a triple-bank shot. "Logical."

9. The Bounty Hunters

"No rules. Just credits."
Shot A: Wretched hive of scum and villainy. A cantina overlooking a dirt pitch.

Shot B: Maradona in a tattered cape with a blaster holster. He winks as he uses his hand to score.

10. The Elders of Gallifrey

"Time is the ultimate playmaker."
Shot A: A stadium inside a giant clock face spinning backwards.

Shot B: Andrea Pirlo sipping wine. The ball is frozen in mid-air. He chips it through a wormhole.
